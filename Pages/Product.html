Urus

The Lamborghini Urus is a high-performance luxury SUV launched in 2018. It combines Lamborghini's signature design with the practicality of an SUV. Powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, it delivers over 640 horsepower. The Urus accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.6 seconds. It offers advanced technology, premium interiors, and off-road capabilities.

Huracan

The Lamborghini Huracán is a sleek, high-performance sports car introduced in 2014. It features a naturally aspirated 5.2-liter V10 engine delivering thrilling power. Known for its sharp design and aerodynamic styling, it's built for speed. The Huracán can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in around 3.2 seconds. It offers a dynamic driving experience with cutting-edge technology and luxury.

Aventador

The Lamborghini Aventador is a flagship supercar known for its bold design and power. It features a 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine producing over 700 horsepower. Launched in 2011, it represents the peak of Lamborghini’s performance and innovation. The Aventador can sprint from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.8 seconds.

Murciélago

The Lamborghini Murciélago is a legendary V12-powered supercar produced from 2001 to 2010. It features a 6.2 to 6.5-liter engine, depending on the model variant, with over 600 horsepower. With its low-slung design and signature scissor doors, it embodies classic Lamborghini style. The Murciélago can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in around 3.3 seconds.
