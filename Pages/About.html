Lamborghini is a luxury sports car manufacturer based in Italy, known for its high-performance, stylish, and exotic vehicles. It was founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini with the aim of competing with established brands like Ferrari. The company is famous for producing iconic models like the Aventador, Huracán, and Urus. Lamborghini cars are characterized by their powerful engines, sharp designs, and advanced technology. Today, Lamborghini is owned by the Volkswagen Group through its Audi division and continues to be a symbol of speed and prestige. The brand uses the raging bull as its logo, symbolizing strength, power, and the founder’s zodiac sign, Taurus. Over the years, Lamborghini has also entered the world of racing and hybrid technology. Its cars are often featured in movies, video games, and luxury collections worldwide. Despite being a niche manufacturer, Lamborghini has a massive global fan base and continues to set benchmarks in the supercar industry.
